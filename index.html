General

Use keys a/d to flip through levels, esc to go to start menu, alt to take a screen shot.

Classic Mode

Use the arrow keys to move the paddle, space to release the ball, p to pause/unpause the game.

Creative Mode

Left click on the screen to add/remove bricks, right click to change the lives the brick has, press ctrl to add blank level, del to delete the level.

Lives: 3
